# AI Builders Summit 2026

# Personalized Welcome Modal Design Specification

Version: 1.0

Purpose:
Create a memorable first impression by greeting visitors with a premium, personalized onboarding experience before they enter the summit website.

---

# 1. Experience Goal

The welcome modal should feel like entering an exclusive technology conference.

It should create curiosity, anticipation, and personalization.

The experience should feel inspired by:

* Apple product launches
* OpenAI experiences
* Linear onboarding
* Premium SaaS onboarding flows

The modal should feel elegant, futuristic, and minimal.

---

# 2. Modal Behavior

## First Visit

When a visitor lands on the website:

1. Entire page loads in the background.
2. Background remains blurred.
3. Welcome modal appears centered.
4. User cannot interact with the page until completing the modal.

---

## Returning Visitor

If a visitor name exists in Local Storage:

Skip the input form.

Show:

"Welcome back, John."

Display for 2 seconds.

Then automatically transition into the website.
