# AI Builders Summit 2026

## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0
Project Type: Premium Event & Conference Website
Platform: Responsive Web Application
Primary Goal: Deliver a world-class conference experience through a visually immersive, animated, and highly engaging one-page website.

---

# 1. Executive Summary

AI Builders Summit 2026 is a premium two-day technology conference focused on Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Automation, and Generative AI.

The website will not be a traditional conference website. Instead, it will be an immersive digital experience that combines cinematic visuals, advanced motion design, storytelling, personalization, and seamless registration.

The experience should evoke the feeling of attending a world-class event similar to major product launches, technology summits, and innovation conferences.

The website will serve as:

* Event showcase
* Registration portal
* Speaker showcase
* Schedule explorer
* Brand-building experience

---

# 2. Product Vision

Create the most memorable conference landing page experience possible while maintaining exceptional performance, accessibility, and usability.

The experience should feel:

* Premium
* Elegant
* Futuristic
* Intelligent
* Interactive
* Smooth
* Modern

Users should feel excitement and anticipation from the moment they land on the page.

The website should rely on storytelling rather than traditional navigation.

# 6. User Journey

## Step 1

Visitor lands on homepage.

## Step 2

Fullscreen welcome modal appears.

Prompt:

"Welcome to AI Builders Summit 2026. What is your name?"

## Step 3

User enters name.

System stores name in Local Storage.

## Step 4

Website personalizes selected content.

Examples:

* Welcome back, John
* John, your AI journey starts here

## Step 5

User explores:

* Hero
* Story
* Speakers
* Schedule
* Venue
* Registration

## Step 6

User submits registration.

## Step 7

Confirmation message appears.
