# AI Builders Summit 2026

# Design Specification

Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Design Philosophy

AI Builders Summit 2026 should feel like a premium technology product launch rather than a conventional conference website.

The experience should combine:

* Apple's visual polish
* OpenAI's elegance
* Stripe's storytelling
* Linear's precision
* Vercel's minimalism

Design Principles:

* Minimal but not empty
* Futuristic but not flashy
* Animated but not distracting
* Premium but approachable
* Mobile-first
* Performance-conscious

The website should guide users through a narrative journey rather than a collection of sections.
